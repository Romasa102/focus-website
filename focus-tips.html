Focus Techniques

Scientifically proven methods to improve concentration

Pomodoro Technique

Work intensely for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After completing four cycles, take a longer 15-30 minute break. This technique leverages the brain's natural focus cycles.

Try it now

Dedicated Space

Create a dedicated area used only for focused work. Your brain will associate this space with productivity, making it easier to concentrate when you're there.

Digital Detox

Silence notifications and put your phone out of sight during study sessions. Each distraction can cost up to 23 minutes of focused attention.

Active Learning

Engage with material by taking notes, teaching concepts to others, or creating mind maps. Active participation increases retention by up to 75%.

Optimal Nutrition

Stay hydrated and avoid heavy meals before study sessions. Foods rich in omega-3 fatty acids, antioxidants, and protein can enhance cognitive function.

Strategic Movement

Take short walks between study sessions to increase blood flow to the brain. Even brief exercise can improve focus for up to 2 hours afterward.
